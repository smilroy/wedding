By Car

If you are coming across the top of Toronto, take Highway 401 to the Leslie Street Exit. Drive south on Leslie until you get to the stoplights at Lawrence Avenue. At this moment in time you have three options:

  1. You may turn right at the intersection and take the first or second driveway on your left to enter Edwards Gardens and find the TBG.
  2. You may proceed straight through the lights and take the first right into the large parking area surrounding the TBG. Parking is offered free of charge.
  3. You may turn left and head towards the Toronto Zoo. This is not recommended if you wish to be on time for our wedding.

By TTC

Exit the Yonge Subway line at the EGLINTON station and take the 51, 54 or 54A bus to LAWRENCE avenue. Yes this confused us too. The bus turns onto Leslie which is supposed to happen. You want to exit at Leslie and Lawrence Ave. The TBG is on the Southwest Corner.

By Bicycle

Make your way into the Sunnybrook and Wilket Creek parts of the City of Toronto Parks system and you will find bicycle and walking paths up to Edwards Gardens. The TBG is in Edwards Gardens, at the corner of Lawrence Avenue and Leslie Street.
