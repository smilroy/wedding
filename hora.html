The Hora

As part of our wedding we are combining our two different backgrounds. One of the dances that is traditional for Jewish Weddings is the Hora. We hope that everyone from both sides of the family will participate, it is extremely fun and very easy. Below are some videos to show you what the hora is like. Don't worry too much about the steps, it is more about moving in the circle and having fun. We would love for everyone to join in the dance at the wedding. For those of you who feel inspired to hoist us up on chairs, we will think light thoughts and lose our water weight the week before.
