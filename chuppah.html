The Chuppah

The chuppah is a wedding canopy which represents the marriage home, portable and open to friends and family. It requires support to hold it up, from the outside by the people in our lives, internally by our love of each other. The bride and groom are brought to the huppah (wedding canopy) by both parents. It is a symbol of the home to be built and shared by the couple. It represents the creation of a new family unit, with parents and friends outside.
